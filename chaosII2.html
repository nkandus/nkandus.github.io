Chaos II

Steel, Neodimion magnets

7' tall, 3' wide

 

This chaotic pendulum works using the attractive and repulsive forces provided by magnets. A magnet attached to the end of the pendulum navigates a magnetic field provided by five other magnets placed on the base. The pendulum swings according to the field created, which can be changed by the viewer at any time.
