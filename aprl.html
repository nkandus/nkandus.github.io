Art and Physics Research Laboratory at UCSC

 

The Art and Physics Research Laboratory at the University of California Santa Cruz, is a place for interdisciplinary research and collaboration between the world of science, mathematics and art. It offers opportunities to create projects that utilize the diverse skill set of the individuals involved, as well as a forum where all parties involved gain stimulation to propel their personal research. The Art & Physics Research Lab was co-founded by Professors Jennifer Parker, Enrico Ramirez-Ruiz and UCSC Art & Phyiscs graduate Nathan Kandus
