Between Dimensions

Projectors, camera, computer, midi pad, metal, projection sceens

30'x50'x20'

In Creation for Burning Man, 2014

 

“Between Dimensions” is a way for the citizens of Burning Man to create and interact with fractals.  To play and experiment is inherently in our nature, and this piece is a platform that allows for the creative exploration of a concept that has given so much to us. To gain a better understanding of such a concept is invaluable, for from the scientific to the esoteric, fractals are a part of our world.
